### Never Assuming Identity
Inviting clients to share their cultural affiliations, beliefs, and sense of identityâ€”honoring their self-definition rather than imposing categories.

Beginning questions with "Correct me if I'm wrong, but..." and sharing my own cultural experiences when appropriate to build understanding.

### Creative Expression Tools

### Community Building

### Specialization from Experience
